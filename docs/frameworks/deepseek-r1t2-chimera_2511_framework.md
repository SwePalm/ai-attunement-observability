# The Friction Frontier: Preserving Humanity in the Age of Algorithmic Optimization

## 1. WAVES OF CHANGE

### Wave 1: Sensory Colonization (2025-2029)
- **Technology**: Ubiquitous AR/VR interfaces, retina-tracking ads, biometric wearables
- **Economic Incentives**: Attention economy scaling to synaptic monetization, "free" ad-supported hardware ecosystems
- **Political Conditions**: Corporate-driven regulatory frameworks for AR urban layers, weak neural data privacy laws
- **Time Window**: 2025-2029 (peak adoption) → Lays neural data infrastructure

### Wave 2: Neural Integration (2027-2034)
- **Technology**: FDA-approved neural implants (e.g., Neuralink 2.0), emotion-sensing wearables
- **Economic Incentives**: Neurocredit scoring systems replacing FICO, corporate neuroplasticity training programs
- **Political Conditions**: "Neuro Rights" legislation debates, corporate lobbying against cognitive liberty protections
- **Time Window**: 2027-2034 → Creates brain-computer interface standards

### Wave 3: Cognitive Offloading (2030-2036)
- **Technology**: AI memory prosthetics, automated decision-making implants
- **Economic Incentives**: Cognitive labor markets replacing gig work, neuromarketing derivatives trading
- **Political Conditions**: Mandatory neural transparency laws, crisis-era emergency cognitive protocols
- **Time Window**: 2030-2036 → Eliminates need for biological cognition in workflows

### Wave 4: Existential Engineering (2032-2039)
- **Technology**: AI-generated purpose/personality algorithms, grief-optimization systems
- **Economic Incentives**: Meaning-as-a-service subscriptions, emotional labor futures markets
- **Political Conditions**: UN restrictions on euphoria-inducing tech, mandatory struggle quotas
- **Time Window**: 2032-2039 → Automates meaning-creation processes

### Wave 5: Biomechanical Symbiosis (2035-2044)
- **Technology**: CRISPR-enhanced neuroadaptation, quantum-AI co-evolution systems
- **Economic Incentives**: Authenticity scarcity markets, bio-crisis adaptation industries
- **Political Conditions**: Splintered governance models (Optimization States vs. Analog Reservations)
- **Time Window**: 2035-2044 → Final stratification of human experience
